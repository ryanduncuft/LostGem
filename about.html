LostGem

Sophie Christoforou (born September 27, 2003), known professionally as LostGem, is a British singer from Croydon, United Kingdom. Known for her distinct vocal style and emotive lyrics, LostGem began gaining attention in the UK music scene in early 2024. She released her debut single, "Perfect," on 27 September 2024, in collaboration with British rapper Ryze Tha Kidd. The single marked the beginning of her professional music career, showcasing her unique blend of Trap, Pop and R&B influences. LostGem continues to build her reputation as an emerging artist in the British music industry, captivating audiences with her soulful voice and compelling performances.

Early Life

Born in Croydon, Sophie grew up with her family. Her early musical influences spanned a diverse range, from pop and R&B to hip hop and trap. Her journey into music commenced in 2019, where she began experimenting with vocals and songwriting. Over time, she has meticulously refined her sound and continues to develop her unique artistic style.

Career

LostGem rapidly established herself through her innovative, genre-blending sound. Her music has consistently evolved, serving as a reflection of her personal experiences and diverse artistic inspirations. As of 2024, she remains a prominent and rising force within the UK music scene, continually releasing impactful projects independently under the label, Ryze Collective Media Group.
